Pro A/V product

These products are being used in TV exhibition, TV store and Digital advertisement market for demonstration use. It makes high resolution video playing/distributing with outstanding quality.

UHD Stream generator

This is capable of transmitting Ultra High definition signals to different TVs upto 8 in Max. simultaneously. It’s possible to support various resolutions and files including up/down-scaling. It’s optimized for professional market such as shops and exhibitors. This device is been fully tested and used in working environment by Global TV manufacturers to display their TVs in worldwide.
